Mitch Altman is co-founder of Noisebridge hackerspace in San Francisco, invented TV-B-Gone, co-founded a Silicon Valley startup , pioneered VR, is an author and teacher, and gives talks and workshops around the world. Mitch promotes hackerspaces, open source hardware, and mentors wherever he goes. He is President and CEO of Cornfield Electronics.
